What each part means

    

      

        Posterior P(A|B)
        What you believe AFTER seeing the evidence. This is what you want to find.
      

      

        Likelihood P(B|A)
        How often does this evidence appear when A is actually true?
      

      

        Prior P(A)
        Your base belief BEFORE seeing any evidence. How common is A in general?
      

      

        False alarm rate
        How often does the evidence appear even when A is NOT true? (P(B|not-A))
